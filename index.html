About

Sandeep is a passionate research scholar at Microwave Remote Sensing Lab (MRSLab), Center of Studies in Resource Engineering, IIT Bombay. His current research focussed on study of vegetation degradation using remote sensing satellite images. His research aims to advance and apply multi-sensor remote sensing datasets for the modeling of vegetation disturbances. He is particularly interested in developing new and robust vegetation parameters using optical and Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) datasets, which will be useful in detecting disturbances in vegetation. The advancement and availability of the global open access high-resolution optical and SAR datasets open the scope of global vegetation monitoring. He looks forward to broaden and deepen his knowledge of geoinformatics, specifically remote sensing of vegetation. Moreover, He wants to employ his expertise and understanding of the subject to better manage forest ecosystems.
